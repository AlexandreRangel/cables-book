### TrackWebcamColor
![TrackWebcamColor op](images/ops/Ops_Extension_Trackingjs_TrackWebcamColor.svg)

**Full Name:** `Ops.Extension.Trackingjs.TrackWebcamColor`

Track a position of a specific color in the current webcam stream.

**`\inputsymbol`{=latex} Inputs**

- **Update** (Trigger)
- **Video Element** (Object)
- **Threshold** (Number)
- **Resize Video** (Number)
- **R** (Number)
- **G** (Number)
- **B** (Number)

**`\outputsymbol`{=latex} Output**

- **Positions** (Array)
- **Sizes** (Array)

**Example:** [cables.gl/edit/vSwLsT](https://cables.gl/edit/vSwLsT)
