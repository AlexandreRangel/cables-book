### OpentypeToSvgPath
![OpentypeToSvgPath op](images/ops/Ops_Extension_OpenType_OpentypeToSvgPath.svg)

**Full Name:** `Ops.Extension.OpenType.OpentypeToSvgPath`

**Description:** get svg path from (OTF) OpentypeFont using the opentype library
